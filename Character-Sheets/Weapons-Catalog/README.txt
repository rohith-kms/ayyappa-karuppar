Weapons & Standards Catalog
===========================
A unified visual reference for every named weapon, shield, and standard
across both armies. Use the named weapons in prompts (e.g., "Dharma Spear",
"Asura War Axe", "Mahishi War Banner") rather than inventing weapon
descriptors.

Master plate: Weapons-and-Standards_catalog.png

The catalog covers:
  DHARMA ARMY:  Spear, Sword, Mace, Bow, Axe, Aruval, Shield, Standard
  ASURA ARMY:   Curved Cleaver, War Axe, War Club, Giant Battle Mace,
                War Shield (lion-headed shield is Mahishi-only),
                War Banner (buffalo-skull standard)

Full descriptors live in /CHARACTER_BIBLE.txt §5.

PER-PROMPT WORKFLOW: when a beat features a named weapon prominently
(close-up of Karuppar's aruval, hero shot of the Ayyappa standard, etc.),
attach this catalog plate as a reference.
