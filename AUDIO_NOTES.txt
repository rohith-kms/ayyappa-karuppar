AUDIO_NOTES.txt — GLOBAL SOUND ARC  (primarily for the EDITING TEAM)
==============================================================================
Per-shot SFX live inside each Sequence's Animation-prompt.txt. This file holds
the GLOBAL audio design that ties the whole reel together. Build the final mix
to this arc.

THE BROKEN-HORN MOTIF  (the emotional spine):
  - 0:00 cold open: a war-horn cracks and FAILS mid-note (despair).
  - 1:10 first strike: the SAME horn sounds again, WHOLE and triumphant (rescue).
  This call-and-answer is the single most important audio cue. Do not cut it.

MUSIC ARC:
  MOVEMENT I  (0:00–0:32) : sparse, low, a slowing heartbeat; dread strings;
                            almost no melody. Let silence and breath dominate.
  MOVEMENT II (0:32–0:50) : near silence; a single sustained pure tone; a
                            building sub-bass under Shiva's close-up.
  MOVEMENT III(0:50–1:14) : the SILENCE (0:56) must be near-total — drop almost
                            everything. Then the ridgeline note rises. The first
                            strike (1:10) is the full orchestral + percussion
                            DROP. This is the peak emotional release.
  MOVEMENT IV (1:14–2:00) : relentless war percussion accelerating with the
                            cuts; choir swells on the aerial chain-storm (1:34);
                            resolve to a triumphant low drone on the final frame.

VOICE-OVER (VO) LINES — in order (record in a deep, grave, measured voice):
  1. "For three days... Dharma held the line."
  2. "They struck them down... and more rose. Courage has a bottom. And they had
      reached it."
  3. "Every wound you gave her... she wore as a crown."
  4. "When Adharma will not stay fallen... Dharma does not answer gently."
  5. "At the edge of the village. At the border of the world. The one who
      watches... had come."
  6. "Enough."  (Karuppar, on-screen, lower/harder than the narrator)
  7. "He did not come like a helper. He came... like a verdict."

KEY DIEGETIC SFX TO FEATURE:
  - ragged breathing (0:10 POV), wet blade impacts, chain whips and rings,
    Shakti-lightning crackle, horse scream + thundering hooves, the ASH "poof"
    on the wind (1:30+), and the final single dog bark on the smash-to-black.

SILENCE IS A SOUND: the two hard-cut-to-black / near-silence beats (0:26 and
0:56) are deliberate. Resist the urge to fill them.
