[SEQUENCE 04 — Mahishi spawns asuras — duration ~8s]

A slow tilt upward from black smoke and darkness, then a signature horror beat of hair-to-asura spawning in macro, then a wide pull-back revealing the multiplied horde.

BEGIN (0:00): Low-angle darkness. Black smoke swirls. We begin a slow, reverent tilt upward, as if gazing at something immense.

REVEAL (2s): Through the smoke, a silhouette emerges — MAHISHI, colossal, arrogant, backlit against a furnace-orange sky. She is LAUGHING, a deep, terrible laugh that echoes across the wasteland.

THE SIGNATURE BEAT (2-6s): In SLOW MOTION, Mahishi rips a fistful of hair from her own arm and CASTS IT TO THE WIND. The camera CUTS to a MACRO CLOSE-UP of a single strand as it hits the wet earth — swells, SPLITS, and UNFOLDS WETLY into a newborn asura, muscle knitting over raw glistening skin, born SCREAMING. Hold on this horror in slow-mo detail.

PULL BACK (6-8s): Camera pulls back to reveal the ridgeline ERUPTING with THOUSANDS of these newborn asuras rising at once, shoulder to shoulder, freshly unfolded, fanged and screaming and endless. An army born from one gesture.

Camera motion: Low-angle slow tilt (0-2s) → macro insert close-up (2-6s, slow-mo) → pull-back wide reveal (6-8s). Composition deliberately moves from intimate horror to overwhelming scale.

Audio note: Mahishi's laugh (0-2s) → wet, organic sounds of spawning, unfurling flesh (2-6s) → sudden chorus of a thousand asura screams (6-8s).
