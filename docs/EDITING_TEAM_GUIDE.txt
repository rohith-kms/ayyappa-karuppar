==============================================================================
EDITING TEAM GUIDE
==============================================================================
Your job: stitch the 22 animated clips into one ~2:00 reel and build the sound.

SETUP: do docs/GITHUB_FOR_BEGINNERS.txt first (one-time).

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WORKFLOW
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. GitHub Desktop: Fetch + Pull to get all the latest animation.mp4 files.
2. ASSEMBLY ORDER = top-to-bottom of STATUS.md = Sequence_01, 02, 03 ... 22.
   Place each Sequences/Sequence_NN/animation.mp4 on the timeline in that order.
3. CUTS: most transitions are HARD CUTS. Two beats are special:
   - 0:26 (Seq 05) ends on a HARD CUT TO BLACK held ~1–2s of silence.
   - 0:56 (Seq 10) is THE SILENCE — drop almost all sound before the ridgeline.
   Read each Animation-prompt's NOTES for its specific transition.
4. SOUND — follow AUDIO_NOTES.txt exactly. The non-negotiables:
   - The BROKEN-HORN MOTIF: cracked/failing horn at 0:00; the SAME horn returns
     WHOLE and triumphant at 1:10 (first strike). This is the emotional payoff.
   - The big music DROP lands on the first strike (1:10), after the silence.
   - Choir/percussion peak on the AERIAL CHAIN-STORM (Seq 18, 1:34) — the money
     shot.
   - Record the 7 VO lines (listed in AUDIO_NOTES.txt) in a deep, grave voice and
     place them on their beats.
5. GRADE: keep Movement I drained/monochrome, Movement II cool blue-white,
   Movements III–IV deep crimson + ash. The colour journey is intentional.
6. TITLES: no logo at the open. End card after the smash-to-black:
   "KARUPPAR — THE GUARDIAN OF DHARMA". Last sound = one distant dog bark.
7. Export 9:16 (per CONFIG) for the reel. Save the final file and project where
   the producer directs (large exports may live on a shared drive, not the repo —
   ask the producer).

TIMING NOTE: the script runs tight against 2:00. If you're over, trim the early
Movement-IV beats (the Plow / Spear Ride) rather than the arrival or the aerial.
