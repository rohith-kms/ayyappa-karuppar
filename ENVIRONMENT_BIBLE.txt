==============================================================================
ENVIRONMENT_BIBLE.txt — LOCKED DESCRIPTORS FOR LOCATIONS
==============================================================================
Parallel to CHARACTER_BIBLE.txt, but for places. Every image prompt whose beat
establishes a location must embed the matching LOCKED BLOCK below verbatim —
this keeps the battlefield, Kailash, Mahishi's hill, and the ridgeline visually
consistent across all sequences they appear in.

The reference plates live in /Environment-Sheets/.

CULTURAL ANCHOR REMINDER:
  Every prompt must also include the GLOBAL_CULTURAL_TOKEN from CONFIG.txt.
  Environments are MOST prone to Western drift — left to itself the model will
  render the battlefield as Helm's Deep, Kailash as a Greek mountain, etc.

Each block has:
  LOCKED BLOCK         = paste this verbatim into prompts
  NEGATIVES (specific) = per-location negative overlay
  REFERENCE            = which environment plate to attach as image reference

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
BATTLEFIELD  (the cursed plain — Movements I, III, IV)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LOCKED BLOCK:
  "a vast ancient South Indian battlefield plain at dusk, ground the color of
  dried blood and cracked black earth, broken Tamil war-chariots and shattered
  spears half-buried in churned mud, guttering low fires casting sick orange
  light, towering pillars of black smoke rising into a storm-dark sky, ash
  drifting upward on the thermals, a desolate plateau ringed by distant
  jagged hills, the field densely packed with the dead and the dying, the
  ground itself seeming to breathe, no European or Mediterranean architecture
  anywhere in frame, the scale epic and mythological"
NEGATIVES (specific):
  "NOT Helm's Deep, NOT Minas Tirith, NOT a Roman colosseum, NOT a Greek
  battlefield, NOT a Viking shore, NOT Western siege equipment (catapults,
  trebuchets, ballistae), NOT stone castle walls, NOT a desert dune, NOT a
  forest clearing, NOT a snowy field"
REFERENCE: Environment-Sheets/Battlefield/Battlefield_master_plate.png (TODO)
MOVEMENT TINTS:
  Movement I  — desaturated to near-monochrome, only sick orange firelight
  Movement III — same plain but with one shaft of pale gold light on the ridge
  Movement IV — full crimson + ember-orange, blood spray, lightning above
